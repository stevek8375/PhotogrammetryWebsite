Hueco Tanks in AR

Talking with the rangers at Hueco Tanks State Park, I was made aware that not only are most of the pictographs require a guide for viewing, but that even some of the unrestricted sites may be difficult to access for people with physical impairments. This app would allow a user to user their phone to not only view some of these sites, but also have a feature to accentuate difficult to see pictographs.

  • Learn More

Climbing App

For rock climbers, part of the challenge is to figure out where to place their hands and feet along a route. There are books and websites that shows the general path, but no further details. So I created a prototype that not only shows how to progress along a crag, but also view it in whatever angle they choose.
