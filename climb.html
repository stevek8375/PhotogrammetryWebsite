Climbing App

This is an exploration of how I created an app to help climbers along specific routes.

  • Get Started

Choosing the boulder

Just north of Bishop is an area filled with many climbing boulders. I was familiar with this rock and had references of how to climb one of the routes.

The climber herself

Besides the boulder itself, I also needed to add a climber to show how to manuever through the route.

Rigging the climber

After finishing the model and textures of the climber, the next stage is to rig it so that it's animatable. Extra features were added to make it easier to animate.

Animating

Using videos of previous climbers for reference, I animated the climber based on hand and foot locations along with body poses.

Putting it all together

Many interactive features were added to this app. The most basic is the play button to watch the climbing animation, but I also added a time slider that the user can scrub to a specific time. Also, there is a speed adjuster that can slow down the animation. There is a button to turn on and off where the holds are located. Lastly, users can scroll, rotate, and zoom the screen with their fingers.
